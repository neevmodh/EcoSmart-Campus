Mathematical Foundation

Our optimization system is built on proven Discrete Mathematics concepts that form the intelligence behind energy savings.

Graph Theory

Modeling campus as G=(V,E) for optimal sensor placement and connectivity analysis using Minimum Spanning Trees.

Probability

Predicting room occupancy with P_r(t) to automate device control based on historical usage patterns.

Recurrence Relations

Forecasting energy consumption with E_n = E_{n-1} + U_n - S_n for continuous optimization.

Boolean Logic

Automation rules like L_r(t) = O_r(t) ∧ C_r(t) for intelligent device control.
